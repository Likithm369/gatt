About GAT NCC

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) unit at Global Academy of Technology is a symbol of discipline, dedication, and nation-building. Our unit operates under the 7/D Company of the 8 Karnataka Battalion NCC, Bangalore ‘B’ Group, Karnataka & Goa Directorate.

Led by our Associate NCC Officer Lt. Saravanan R, and guided by experienced PI Staff, GAT NCC trains cadets in leadership, physical fitness, weapon handling, social service, and national pride. From participating in RDC, TSC, Trekking, and Environmental campaigns, our cadets embody the motto: "Unity and Discipline."

We don't just wear the uniform — we live the spirit of NCC every day.

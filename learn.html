What is NCC?

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement. It aims to develop character, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, and spirit of adventure and ideals of selfless service among young citizens.

History of NCC

Established on 16 July 1948, the NCC was formed by merging the University Officers Training Corps. It is governed by the Ministry of Defence and serves as a tri-services organization: Army, Navy, and Air Force.

NCC Wings

  • 🪖 Army Wing – Physical training, drill, map reading, field craft
  • ⚓ Navy Wing – Seamanship, sailing, navigation
  • ✈️ Air Wing – Flight simulation, aero modeling, flying basics

Certificates in NCC

  • 🎓 Certificate A – After 1 year in school NCC
  • 🎓 Certificate B – After 2 years in college NCC
  • 🎓 Certificate C – Final and most valuable; required for army/defense exams

Trainings Conducted

  • 📏 Drill & Parade Training
  • 🔫 Weapon Handling & Firing
  • 🧭 Map Reading
  • 🏞 Camps: ATC, CATC, RDC, TSC, BLC, EBSB, NIC
  • 🎤 Leadership & Cultural Activities

Benefits of Joining NCC

  • ✅ Priority in defense jobs
  • ✅ Scholarships and reservation in universities
  • ✅ Improves physical fitness and leadership
  • ✅ Participation in national-level camps
  • ✅ Personal transformation through discipline

How to Join NCC?

Students can enroll at the beginning of the academic year through the official NCC Unit. Selection is done through physical test and interview. Forms are shared on the official website during enrollment season.


Camps You Can Attend as an NCC Cadet

🏕️ ATC: Annual Training Camp - Basic parade & PT
⚔️ CATC: Combined Annual Training Camp - Combined unit training
🇮🇳 RDC: Republic Day Camp (Delhi) - Top cadet national level parade
🪖 TSC: Thal Sainik Camp - Shooting, map reading, physical challenge
🧭 BLC: Basic Leadership Camp - Team command and leadership training
🎖 ALC: Advanced Leadership Camp - High-level team missions and command
🤝 NIC: National Integration Camp - Unity in diversity across states
🌍 EBSB: Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat - Cultural exchange between cadets
📍 IGC: Inter-Group Competition - Pre-RDC/TSC selection stage
🫡 Pre-RDC: Screening for Delhi Republic Day Camp
💪 Pre-TSC: Weapon training, map test, physical grind
🏞️ SNIC: Special NIC - Exclusive for selected cadets
🌐 YEP: Youth Exchange Programme - Represent India abroad
🎯 RDC DG: Parade before DG NCC - final showcase
